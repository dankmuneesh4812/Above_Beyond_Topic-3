Volvo XC90

The Volvo XC90 is a mid-size luxury crossover SUV manufactured and marketed by Volvo Cars since 2002 and now in its second generation. The first generation was introduced at the 2002 North American International Auto Show and used the Volvo P2 platform shared with the first generation Volvo S80 and other large Volvo cars.

The XC90 was redesigned for the 2016 model year. Since then, the model has undergone incremental changes, such as the addition of an optional third-row seat for the 2018 model year, and a mild hybrid powertrain option for the 2020 model year.

The XC90 is available in multiple engine options, including a plug-in hybrid version called the T8. It also offers a number of advanced safety features, including adaptive cruise control, lane departure warning, and automatic emergency braking.

The Volvo XC90 is a luxury SUV with plenty of space for up to seven passengers. It comes with a variety of features to enhance your driving experience, including:

  • 360-Degree Surround View Camera
  • Pilot Assist with Adaptive Cruise Control
  • Blind Spot Information System with Steer Assist and Cross Traffic Alert
  • Run-off Road Mitigation and Protection
  • Harman Kardon Premium Sound System

In addition to these features, the XC90 also has several smart features designed to make your life easier:

  • Remote Start with Climate Control
  • Volvo On Call App
  • Sensus Navigation
  • Apple CarPlay and Android Auto
  • Wireless Charging
