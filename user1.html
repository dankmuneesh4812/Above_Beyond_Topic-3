Tesla Model 3

The Tesla Model 3 is a compact electric sedan with cutting-edge technology and impressive performance. The car is capable of accelerating from 0 to 60 mph in just 5.3 seconds and has a top speed of 140 mph.

The Model 3 is equipped with an advanced autopilot system that enables the car to automatically steer, accelerate and brake for other vehicles and pedestrians within its lane. The system also includes a number of safety features such as automatic emergency braking, collision warning, and blind spot monitoring.

Charging and Range

The Model 3 is powered by a lithium-ion battery pack that provides a range of up to 263 miles on a single charge. The car can be charged using a standard 120-volt outlet, a 240-volt outlet, or a Tesla Supercharger. Superchargers can provide up to 170 miles of range in just 30 minutes of charging.

Design Features

  • The Model 3's sleek and minimalist design is not only aesthetically pleasing, but also contributes to its impressive aerodynamics and efficiency.
  • The car's exterior is made from a combination of lightweight aluminum and high-strength steel, while the interior features premium materials and a large touchscreen display.
  • The Model 3's battery pack is located under the floor, which not only lowers the center of gravity and improves handling, but also frees up interior space.
  • The car's minimalist interior design is centered around a large 15-inch touchscreen display, which controls most of the car's functions including navigation, media, and climate control.

Smart Car Features

  • Autopilot advanced safety and convenience features are designed to assist you with the most burdensome parts of driving.
  • Full LED lighting with 14 three-position dynamic turning lights provides better visibility at night.
  • Full Self-Driving capability provides automatic driving on city streets and highways pending regulatory approval, as well as automatic parking and summon.
  • High-efficiency particulate air (HEPA) filtration system removes at least 99.97% of particulate exhaust pollution and effectively all allergens, bacteria and other contaminants from cabin air.
  • The Model 3 is also equipped with a range of other features including a panoramic glass roof, premium audio system, and keyless entry.
